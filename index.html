Privacy Policy - Jigsaw Puzzle: Kudaketa Kakera

Last updated:11/30/2025

1. Introduction

Jigsaw Puzzle: Kudaketa Kakera (“the Game”) is developed by Sigkill Studios. We respect your privacy. This Game is designed so that we do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The Game does not collect or store your name, email address, phone number, age, photos, videos, audio, contacts, calendar information, location (precise), financial data, health data, or any personal identifiers. We do not have user accounts and do not run any servers.

3. Third-Party Services

a) Google AdMob (Advertising)

The Game uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (city-level)
  • App interactions
  • Diagnostics and crash information
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected and processed by Google to display ads, measure ad performance, and improve ad quality.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Google Play Billing

The Game uses Google Play Billing for the optional “Remove Ads” purchase. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment or purchase data.

4. Support Contact

The Game includes a Support button that opens your email app with a pre-filled email. This is a manual, user-initiated action. You may edit or remove any information before sending. We only receive this information if you choose to send the email.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for general audiences. We do not collect personal data from children.

6. Security

All data handled by Google AdMob or Google Play is encrypted in transit and protected with industry-standard security. The Game itself does not transmit or store any data.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
