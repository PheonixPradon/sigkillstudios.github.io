Privacy Policy - Jigsaw Puzzle: Kudaketa Kakera

Last updated: 12/20/2025

1. Introduction

Jigsaw Puzzle: Kudaketa Kakera (“the Game”) is developed by Sigkill Studios. We respect your privacy. This Game is designed so that we do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users beyond what is handled by trusted third-party services.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The Game does not collect or store your name, email address, phone number, age, photos, videos, audio recordings, contacts, calendar information, precise or approximate location, financial data, health data, or any sensitive personal identifiers. We do not maintain user accounts and do not run any servers.

3. Third-Party Services

a) Google Play Games Services (Achievements & Login)

The Game uses Google Play Games Services to provide optional features such as achievements and cloud-based player identity. When you sign in, Google Play Games may provide:

  • A unique Player ID
  • Optional display name and profile image (read-only)

We do not store, share, or transmit this information. Google handles all authentication and account management. Google Play Games Services does not provide location data to the Game.

Google Play Games Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Google AdMob (Advertising)

The Game uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (city-level)
  • App interactions
  • Diagnostics and crash information
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected and processed by Google to display ads, measure ad performance, and improve ad quality. We do not collect or store this data ourselves.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

c) Google Play Billing

The Game uses Google Play Billing for the optional “Remove Ads” purchase. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment details, card information, or personal financial data. The Game only receives a purchase confirmation token to enable the “Remove Ads” feature.

4. Support Contact

The Game includes a Support button that opens your email app with a pre-filled email. This is a manual, user-initiated action. You may edit or remove any information before sending. We only receive this information if you choose to send the email.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Security

All data handled by Google AdMob, Google Play Games Services, and Google Play Billing is encrypted in transit and protected with industry-standard security. The Game itself does not transmit, upload, or store any user data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page.
